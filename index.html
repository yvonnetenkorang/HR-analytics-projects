Projects

Retail Performance Analysis using Power BI

Aurora Threads Boutique is a growing high-end fashion retailer with multiple branches, and they wanted to better use their business data to improve decision-making and performance. In this analysis, I used Power Query for data cleaning and transformation, DAX for analytical logic and custom measures, and Power BI for dashboard development. The project focused on improving visibility into retail performance and demonstrating how analytics can support stronger business decisions in a competitive fashion environment.

Business Performance Analysis using Excel

Café Harmony wanted to better understand its sales performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency. Using Excel, Pivot Tables I built a dashboard to analyze revenue trends, menu performance, customer demographics, inventory management, employee performance, and customer feedback. The analysis showed that muffins and cappuccinos were top revenue drivers, middle-aged customers contributed the largest share of sales, and some key ingredients were running low while others were overstocked. This project demonstrates how Excel dashboards can support smarter decisions around marketing, stock planning, and service improvement.

Cinema Revenue & Ticket Sales Performance Analysis using Excel

FilMax Cinemas is a growing movie theatre chain operating across multiple U.S. states. The company wanted better visibility into its ticket sales performance, customer demand patterns, and revenue trends across branches. In this project, I analyzed cinema transaction data and built an interactive Excel dashboard to evaluate ticket sales, revenue performance, movie popularity, and customer demand across showtimes and locations. The dashboard highlights key metrics such as total revenue, ticket volume, weekday performance, and revenue contribution by ticket category. It also analyzes movie performance and identifies peak showtimes that drive higher ticket sales. These insights help management understand which movies attract the most audiences, which ticket categories generate the most revenue, and how demand changes across different days and showtimes. The dashboard enables data-driven decisions on movie scheduling, pricing strategies, and promotional campaigns to maximize cinema revenue.

Moving from Chaos to Clarity: Introducing the Merit Pay Effectiveness and Fairness Process

Merit pay is one of the most widely used compensation practices in organizations, yet many systems fail to produce the motivation and performance outcomes they are designed to achieve. This research examines why merit pay programs often fall short and how compensation design, implementation quality, and employee perceptions influence their effectiveness. The study introduces the Merit Pay Effectiveness and Fairness (MPEF) process and framework, offering a structured perspective on how merit pay systems are designed, implemented, and experienced by employees. The research was accepted for presentation at the Eastern Academy of Management Conference.
